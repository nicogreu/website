Research Consulting to Nonprofits

As a consultant, I provide nonprofit and mission-driven organizations with high-level support across the full research lifecycle.

Consulting style: Short & Intense

Rather than long-term, open-ended contracts, I specialize in short, intensive consulting sprints. For a short period of time (typically a few days to a couple of weeks), I work as an extension of your team to help resolve a specific research-related challenge.

Why work with me?

Because I offer a rare combination of deep technical expertise and communicative clarity and appeal.
