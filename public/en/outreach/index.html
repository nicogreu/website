I have a dream that one day a blockbuster movie will invest a large portion of its budget in truly thoughtful educational content.

Until that happens, my comic crish — a semi-fantastic story filled with absurd humor and brief insights about statistics and the research world — is my humble attempt to show that entertaining stories can also convey deep philosophical and educational ideas.

The story of crish is ongoing, and in the next chapters, I plan to weave in some of the results of my current research.

The project has been supported by two grants from the research network GDR EcoStat and the Université Grenoble Alpes.
